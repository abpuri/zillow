Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlipIQ

How is FlipIQ different from PropStream or DealMachine?
PropStream and DealMachine are search toolsyou have to actively look for opportunities. FlipIQ is an intelligence platform with AI agents that proactively find opportunities and alert you. We analyze entire markets and predict where opportunities are emerging, not just show you what already exists. Think of it as the difference between fishing with a rod (them) and fishing with a net (us).
How often is the data updated?
Our data is updated monthly when Zillow releases new research data. Our AI agents process this data within 24 hours and recalculate all opportunity scores. You'll be notified immediately when new high-potential opportunities emerge. For production deployment, we're working on integrating MLS data for weekly or daily updates.
What markets are covered?
FlipIQ covers 26,000+ ZIP codes across all 50 US states. We analyze any market with sufficient data to calculate reliable opportunity scores. This includes most urban, suburban, and many rural markets. You can filter by state, metro area, price range, and score threshold to focus on your target areas.
Do you integrate with my CRM?
Enterprise plan includes API access and custom integrations. We can connect with popular CRMs like Podio, REsimpli, and InvestorFuse. For Starter and Professional plans, you can export data to CSV and import manually. Full CRM integrations are on our roadmap for Q2 2025.
What's the pricing? Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to Professional features. No credit card required to start. After the trial, plans start at $49/month for Starter, $149/month for Professional (most popular), and $499/month for Enterprise. Annual plans save 20%.
How accurate is the scoring model?
Our 5-factor scoring model is backtested against 3 years of historical data. In testing, ZIPs with scores above 70 consistently show 5%+ appreciation and sub-45-day liquidity. The model combines appreciation trends, market velocity, distress signals, pricing power, and value gapsfactors that have historically predicted successful flip markets.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with a few clicks. If you choose an annual plan, you'll save 20% and can still cancel (though refunds are prorated). We're confident you'll see value, which is why we offer a 14-day free trial.
